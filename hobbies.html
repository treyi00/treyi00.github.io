What does he like?

Jiujitsu

Brazilian jiujitsu is one of my favorite sports. Not only is it a good exercise, it also requires critical thinking and strategy, so you get to train both your mind and body.

Traveling

So many wonderful places, so it's always a great joy to be able to see them in person. I enjoy seeing the different facets of the world. I have a preference for experiencing nature more than exploring big cities though.

Foraging

Something about being able to identify, collect, and eat foraged goods is just so fulfilling. Of course, you can buy fruits at the store, but there are some fruits you can only get from foraging like these mulberries.
