Who's this guy?

Education

I went to the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign where I got a bachelor's degree in linguistics and a certificate in undergraduate research and another in computer science fundamentals. I focused on speech processing and did research in acoustic phonetics, phonology, digital signal processing, and human-computer interaction.

Background

I was born in the Philippines, but my entire family moved to the United States when I was in elementary school. I consider both the Philippines and the United States my home. I visit the Philippines regularly and like to do island hopping there—I'm not that good of a swimmer though! On the other hand, I'm mostly a homebody when I'm in the United States.

Values

It sounds very cliché, but I really value kindness and empathy. There are so many bad things that are happening in the world, so I think that the least we can do is not add more negativity to our surroundings. I try to be understanding of others and operate with the belief that the majority of people want to do good and be the protagonist of their own story. Life becomes so much better if you try to see the good in others.
