Education

I went to the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign where I got a bachelor's degree in linguistics and a certificate in undergraduate research and another in computer science fundamentals. I focused on speech processing and did research in acoustic phonetics, phonology, digital signal processing, and human-computer interaction.
